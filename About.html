RESILIENCE ACTION INTERNATIONAL

Resilience Action international(RAI)is a dynamic refugee-led non-govermental organization headquatered in kakuma refugee camp.founded in 2010,Resilience Action international began as a refugee self-help group called solidarity and advocacy with vulnerable individuals in crisis (SAVIC)with a mission to bridge the language gap to employement oppotunities for non-English. over the years,our dedication to empowering communities has driven our transformation from a community-based organization in 2024to an NGO in 2016

Today,RAI stands as a leading refugee-led organization in kakuma,focusing on empowering refugee youth in alignment with the Uneted Nations sustainable development Goals(SDGs)for 2030.RAI'work primarily contributes to targets related to Quality Education,GENDER Equality,Decent work and Economic Growth,and Eeduced inequalities
